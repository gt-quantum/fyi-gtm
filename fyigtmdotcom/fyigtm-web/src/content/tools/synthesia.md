Synthesia enables you to create professional AI videos with virtual presenters, eliminating the need for cameras, actors, or studios.

## What is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video generation platform that creates videos featuring realistic AI avatars speaking your script. It's widely used for training videos, marketing content, and corporate communications.

## Key Features

- 140+ AI avatars to choose from
- 120+ language support
- Custom avatar creation
- Text-to-video generation
- Screen recording integration
- Brand customization

## Pricing

Synthesia plans start at $30/month for personal use, with business plans offering more features and avatar options.

Invideo is an AI-powered video creation platform that helps you make professional-quality videos in minutes, even without prior video editing experience.

## What is Invideo?

Invideo simplifies video creation with AI-powered tools, templates, and an intuitive editor. It's ideal for marketers, content creators, and businesses looking to produce engaging video content quickly.

## Key Features

- AI script-to-video generation
- 5000+ customizable templates
- Stock media library
- Text-to-speech voiceovers
- Brand kit integration
- Social media optimized exports

## What is Momentum?

Momentum is an AI Revenue Orchestration Platform that transforms customer interactions into structured go-to-market data and automatically executes revenue-driving workflows in real-time. Unlike traditional sales tools that simply analyze and report, Momentum acts as an intelligent orchestration layer that captures buyer signals from calls and meetings, then immediately updates CRMs, triggers playbooks, and sends alerts to keep deals moving forward without manual intervention.

## What the Tool Does

Momentum operates as an intelligent command center for revenue teams, sitting between your existing tools to automate the tedious work that typically slows down deals. When a sales rep finishes a call, Momentum's AI automatically analyzes the conversation, extracts key insights like next steps and objections, updates Salesforce fields, and triggers appropriate workflows based on what happened.

The platform transforms Slack into a revenue operations hub where deal rooms appear automatically for important opportunities. When buyer signals are detected during calls, relevant stakeholders across departments receive instant alerts with context about what just happened and what actions need to be taken. This eliminates the delay between customer interactions and team responses that often derails momentum in complex sales cycles.

Teams use Momentum to automate everything from basic CRM hygiene to sophisticated deal recovery sequences. The platform learns from each interaction to improve its recommendations and automate increasingly complex workflows over time.

## Key Capabilities

### Intelligent CRM Automation
Momentum automatically updates Salesforce fields, logs call notes, and maintains data quality without any manual input from sales reps. The AI understands sales contexts and extracts relevant information from conversations to populate the right fields with accurate data. This capability typically cuts Salesforce update time in half while improving data reliability across the entire revenue organization.

### Real-Time Signal Detection and Routing
The platform continuously monitors customer interactions for buying signals, objections, competitive mentions, and other revenue-critical moments. When these signals are detected, Momentum immediately routes alerts to the appropriate team members via Slack with full context about what triggered the notification. This ensures that important opportunities get immediate attention from the right people.

### Automated Workflow Orchestration
Momentum triggers sophisticated multi-step playbooks based on conversation outcomes and CRM changes. These workflows can span multiple tools and departments, automatically coordinating activities like champion development, technical evaluations, and contract negotiations. The orchestration capability allows revenue teams to scale personalized engagement without proportionally increasing headcount.

## Pricing Reality

Momentum operates on annual subscription pricing with three main tiers designed for different organizational needs.

- **Entry cost:** Business plan at $69/user/month requires annual commitment, making the minimum realistic investment around $8,280 for a 10-person team
- **Scaling cost:** Transformation plan at $99/user/month adds advanced workflow capabilities, while Enterprise pricing varies based on customization needs
- **Contract terms:** Annual commitments required for published pricing with custom Enterprise agreements for larger deployments

The platform offers a free trial period, though the complexity of integration and setup means teams need several weeks to properly evaluate the full value proposition. Most organizations require dedicated RevOps resources to maximize their investment, which should be factored into the total cost of ownership.

Acoust is an AI-powered audio enhancement platform that helps podcasters and content creators achieve professional sound quality.

## What is Acoust?

Acoust uses AI to automatically enhance audio recordings, removing background noise, improving clarity, and normalizing levels. It's designed to make podcast and video audio sound professionally produced.

## Key Features

- AI noise removal
- Audio enhancement
- Level normalization
- Echo reduction
- Batch processing
- Multiple export formats

## Pricing

Acoust offers a free tier for basic use. Pro plans start at $9/month for more processing time and advanced features.

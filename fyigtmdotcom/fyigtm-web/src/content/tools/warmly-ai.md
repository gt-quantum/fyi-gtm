## What is Warmly AI?

Warmly AI is an AI-driven sales platform that transforms anonymous website visitors into actionable sales opportunities by identifying both companies and individuals browsing your site in real-time. The platform combines person-level intent detection with automated engagement tools to help go-to-market teams capture and convert high-intent prospects while they're actively showing buying signals.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Not ideal for:** Small businesses, startups with limited budgets, or companies without dedicated SDR functions
- **Typical team size:** 25+ employees with structured sales and marketing operations
- **Time to value:** 2-4 weeks for basic implementation, 6-8 weeks for full optimization
- **Budget reality:** Minimum $18,000 annually, realistically $24,000+ for comprehensive features

## What the Tool Does

Warmly AI operates as a comprehensive visitor intelligence and engagement platform that sits on your website to identify anonymous traffic and convert it into sales opportunities. The system works by analyzing website visitor behavior in real-time, matching visitors against extensive B2B databases to reveal not just company information, but specific individual identities including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and detailed professional profiles.

Once a visitor is identified, Warmly's AI engine evaluates intent signals based on pages visited, time spent, and engagement patterns. When high-intent prospects are detected, the platform can automatically trigger personalized engagement through multiple channels. The AI chat functionality can initiate conversations, ask qualifying questions, provide relevant resources, and even schedule meetings directly with sales representatives.

The platform also sends instant alerts to sales teams via Slack when priority prospects visit the website, enabling immediate follow-up while the prospect is still actively engaged. This real-time notification system significantly reduces response times and increases the likelihood of meaningful conversations with in-market buyers.

## Key Capabilities

### Person-Level Website De-anonymization
Warmly identifies individual visitors beyond just company-level data, providing granular contact information including personal and professional email addresses, phone numbers, and comprehensive profile data. This capability goes far beyond traditional IP-based identification, offering enterprise-grade compliance while delivering actionable contact intelligence that sales teams can immediately act upon.

### AI-Powered Conversational Engagement
The platform's AI chat system can be fully trained to match your brand's tone and messaging while automatically engaging website visitors with qualifying questions and relevant information. The AI can handle complex conversations, offer appropriate collateral based on visitor behavior, and seamlessly book meetings with the right sales representatives, creating a personalized experience that drives higher conversion rates.

### Real-Time Intent Signal Processing
Warmly continuously monitors and analyzes visitor behavior patterns to identify buying intent signals as they happen. The platform processes multiple data points including page visits, content engagement, return frequency, and interaction depth to score and prioritize prospects, ensuring sales teams focus their efforts on the highest-probability opportunities when timing is most critical.

## Pricing Reality

Warmly AI positions itself firmly in the enterprise market with pricing that reflects its comprehensive capabilities and target audience of established B2B organizations.

- **Entry cost:** $18,000 annually for the AI Inbound Agent plan with 10,000 credits
- **Scaling cost:** $24,000 annually for AI Outbound Agent with advanced automation features
- **Contract terms:** Annual commitments required, with credits determining usage limits

The pricing includes access to multiple data provider databases and integrations with major CRM platforms. While there's no free tier or month-to-month options, the platform does offer demos and proof-of-concept implementations for qualified prospects. Organizations should budget for implementation support and potential custom integrations depending on their tech stack complexity.

## What is Oliv?

Oliv is an AI-native revenue intelligence platform that consolidates what traditionally required three separate tools into one unified system. The platform analyzes every deal in your pipeline with autonomous AI agents, providing real-time intelligence and automated workflows so sales teams can focus on strategy and closing rather than administrative tasks.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market to enterprise sales teams drowning in vendor sprawl and looking to consolidate their revenue tech stack
- **Not ideal for:** Small teams under 25 reps or companies wanting basic CRM functionality without AI automation
- **Typical team size:** 50+ sales professionals across multiple teams
- **Time to value:** 2-4 weeks for initial setup, full value realized within 60 days
- **Budget reality:** Significant upfront investment but roughly half the total cost of comparable three-vendor solutions

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Pipeline & Forecasting
- **Pricing model:** Enterprise with modular options
- **Standout capability:** Autonomous AI agents that work in the background without manual intervention

## What the Tool Does

Oliv operates as your sales team's AI-powered intelligence layer, automatically capturing and analyzing every customer interaction across your entire revenue lifecycle. The platform runs continuously in the background, with AI agents that autonomously join sales calls, process conversations within five minutes, and generate contextually accurate deal assessments without requiring manual review.

Rather than forcing sales reps to update spreadsheets or input data manually, Oliv's two-layer architecture captures everything automatically. The Deal Driver AI Agent then synthesizes this information into weekly pipeline reviews that land directly in managers' inboxes, complete with AI commentary explaining why deals moved, which ones are at risk, and what actions need attention.

The platform transforms how revenue teams approach forecasting by removing the typical rep bias that keeps most organizations stuck at 65% forecast accuracy. Through continuous deal analysis and pattern recognition, teams using Oliv report achieving 92% forecast accuracy.

## Key Capabilities

### Autonomous Deal Intelligence
Oliv's AI agents analyze every deal continuously, identifying patterns and risk factors without human intervention. The system generates MEDDIC scorecards automatically and provides weekly breakdowns of pipeline health with clear explanations for deal movement. This eliminates the manual work of deal reviews while providing deeper insights than traditional approaches.

### Real-Time Pipeline Visibility
The platform offers a complete, customizable pipeline view that updates in real-time as new data flows in. Sales leaders can configure columns to display the most relevant metrics for their team while maintaining visibility across the entire customer lifecycle from initial pipeline creation through expansion opportunities.

### Meeting Intelligence and Automation

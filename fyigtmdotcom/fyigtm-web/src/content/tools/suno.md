Suno is a groundbreaking AI music generation platform that creates complete songs with vocals and instrumentation from simple text prompts.

## What is Suno?

Suno uses advanced AI to generate full musical compositions including vocals, lyrics, and instrumentals. Users can create songs in various genres and styles just by describing what they want.

## Key Features

- Full song generation with vocals
- Multiple genre support
- Lyric generation
- Instrumental creation
- Style customization
- Song extension capabilities

## Pricing

Suno offers free daily credits for song generation. Paid subscriptions provide more credits and additional features.

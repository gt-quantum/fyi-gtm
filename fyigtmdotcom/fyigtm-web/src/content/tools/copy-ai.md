Copy.ai is an AI-powered copywriting assistant that helps marketers and businesses create compelling content quickly.

## What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai uses artificial intelligence to generate marketing copy, social media posts, blog content, and more. It's designed to help overcome writer's block and speed up content creation.

## Key Features

- Multiple content templates
- Blog post generation
- Social media copy
- Product descriptions
- Email copy assistance
- Brand voice customization

## Pricing

Copy.ai offers a free tier with limited words. Pro plans start at $49/month for unlimited words and advanced features.

## What is Saleshandy?

Saleshandy is an AI-powered cold email platform that combines lead generation, email automation, and deliverability optimization into a single solution. It helps businesses scale their outbound sales efforts by automating the entire cold email process, from finding verified leads to tracking campaign performance and managing follow-up sequences.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Small to mid-size sales teams looking to scale cold email outreach with AI assistance
- **Not ideal for:** Large enterprises needing complex CRM integrations or companies wanting basic email marketing without sales focus
- **Typical team size:** 5-50 people
- **Time to value:** 1-2 weeks to see initial results from campaigns
- **Budget reality:** $25/month minimum, scales with advanced features and usage needs

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Sales Engagement Platform
- **Pricing model:** Paid subscription with unlimited users and inboxes
- **Standout capability:** AI Sequence CoPilot that builds complete email campaigns from just a website URL

## What the Tool Does

Saleshandy streamlines cold email outreach by handling the technical complexity that typically slows down sales teams. Users upload prospect lists or search the built-in database of 750+ million contacts, then let the AI create personalized email sequences. The platform manages email deliverability, tracks opens and replies in real-time, and automatically sends follow-ups based on prospect behavior. Sales reps receive instant notifications when prospects engage, allowing for timely responses while the automation handles the heavy lifting of maintaining consistent outreach cadence.

The workflow centers around campaigns rather than individual emails. Teams set up sequences once, then the platform runs them continuously, warming up email accounts to maintain sender reputation and adjusting delivery timing to maximize inbox placement. This approach transforms cold email from a manual, time-intensive process into a scalable system that runs in the background.

## Key Capabilities

### AI Sequence CoPilot
The platform's AI can generate complete email sequences by analyzing a prospect's website and company information. Users simply provide a URL and prospect list, and the system creates personalized subject lines, email content, and follow-up messaging. This eliminates the blank page problem that slows down campaign creation and ensures consistent messaging across large prospect lists.

### B2B Lead Database and Management
Saleshandy includes access to over 750 million verified contacts with advanced filtering capabilities. Users can search by company size, industry, job title, and location to build targeted prospect lists. The system handles lead management by tracking engagement history and preventing duplicate outreach, essential for maintaining professional relationships at scale.

### Email Deliverability and Analytics
The platform includes email warming capabilities and deliverability monitoring to keep messages out of spam folders. Real-time analytics track open rates, reply rates, and click-through rates across campaigns, with detailed reporting on individual prospect engagement. This data helps teams refine their messaging and identify the most effective outreach strategies.

## Pricing Reality

Saleshandy starts at $25 per month with unlimited users and email accounts, making it accessible for small teams. However, the pricing structure includes daily limits that may not align with the "unlimited" marketing messaging.

- **Entry cost:** $25/month for basic features with daily sending limits
- **Scaling cost:** Higher tiers remove restrictions and add advanced features
- **Contract terms:** Monthly and annual billing available

The platform imposes a daily limit of 500 emails and caps prospect uploads at 5,000 contacts, which some users find restrictive given the unlimited positioning. Teams planning high-volume campaigns should factor these constraints into their decision.

Runway is a cutting-edge AI creative suite offering advanced video editing, generation, and visual effects tools for filmmakers and content creators.

## What is Runway?

Runway provides a suite of AI-powered creative tools including Gen-2 for text-to-video generation, advanced video editing features, and professional visual effects capabilities.

## Key Features

- Text-to-video generation (Gen-2)
- Image-to-video conversion
- AI-powered video editing
- Green screen and background removal
- Motion tracking
- Color grading and effects

## Pricing

Runway offers a free tier with limited credits. Pro plans start at $15/month with more generation credits and features.

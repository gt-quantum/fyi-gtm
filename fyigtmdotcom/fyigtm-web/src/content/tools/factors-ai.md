## What is Factors AI?

Factors AI is an AI-powered B2B demand generation platform designed to solve one of marketing's most persistent challenges: identifying and engaging anonymous website visitors who are actively researching your product. The platform combines account identification, intent detection, and automated campaign execution to help B2B teams convert more of their website traffic into qualified pipeline.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market and enterprise B2B companies with complex sales cycles and significant website traffic
- **Not ideal for:** Small teams, transactional businesses, or companies focused primarily on email-based outreach
- **Typical team size:** Marketing teams of 5+ people with dedicated demand generation roles
- **Time to value:** 2-4 weeks for initial account identification, 1-2 months for full ABM campaign optimization
- **Budget reality:** Expect $5,000-$12,000+ annually for meaningful functionality

- **Category:** ABM & Demand Generation

## What the Tool Does

Factors AI operates as a comprehensive demand generation engine that transforms anonymous website traffic into actionable marketing opportunities. The platform continuously monitors your website visitors, matching IP addresses to company databases and enriching visitor data with intent signals from across the web.

On a typical day, marketing teams use Factors AI to receive automated alerts when high-value target accounts visit their website, track which content these accounts engage with, and automatically add interested prospects to retargeting campaigns on LinkedIn and Google. The platform's AI agents handle the technical execution of building audiences, launching campaigns, and optimizing targeting parameters based on engagement data.

The workflow typically starts with the platform identifying which companies are visiting your website, even when individuals don't fill out forms. It then layers on intent data from multiple sources to score accounts based on their likelihood to purchase. Marketing teams can set up automated workflows that trigger specific actions when accounts reach certain engagement thresholds, such as adding them to targeted advertising campaigns or alerting sales teams.

## Key Capabilities

### Account Identification and Intelligence
Factors AI reveals the companies behind anonymous website visitors using sophisticated IP intelligence technology. The platform claims to identify over 75% of B2B website traffic, providing details about company size, industry, and technology stack. This capability addresses the fundamental challenge that most B2B buyers research extensively before ever engaging directly with vendors.

### Intent-Driven Account Scoring
The platform aggregates intent signals from multiple sources beyond just website behavior, including social media engagement, content consumption patterns, and third-party intent data providers. These signals feed into AI-powered scoring algorithms that rank accounts based on their likelihood to purchase, helping teams prioritize their outreach and advertising spend on the most promising opportunities.

### Automated ABM Campaign Execution
Through its AdPilot features for LinkedIn and Google, Factors AI automates the creation and management of account-based advertising campaigns. The platform builds audiences based on identified accounts and intent signals, launches targeted campaigns, and continuously optimizes targeting and messaging based on engagement data. This reduces the manual work typically required to execute sophisticated ABM programs.

## Pricing Reality

Factors AI operates on a tiered pricing model with annual contract requirements for most functionality:

- **Entry cost:** Free plan limited to 200 companies per month and 3 user seats
- **Scaling cost:** Basic plan at $399/month (3,000 companies, 5 seats), Growth plan at $999/month (8,000 companies, 10 seats)
- **Contract terms:** Annual contracts required for paid plans, with Enterprise pricing starting around $30,000+ annually

The free plan provides enough functionality to test the platform's account identification capabilities, but serious demand generation programs will quickly exceed the 200-company limit. Most mid-market companies should expect to pay $5,000-$12,000 annually, while enterprise implementations often reach $30,000+ based on account volume and feature requirements.

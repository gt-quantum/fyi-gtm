## What is Attio: The next gen of CRM?

Attio is a modern, AI-native CRM platform that reimagines customer relationship management with extreme flexibility and intuitive design. Built from the ground up for today's agile businesses, it offers a modular, no-code approach to tracking leads and managing relationships while promising to adapt to any business's unique operational reality rather than forcing companies into rigid templates.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Startups and growing businesses (10-50 team members) needing highly customizable CRM workflows
- **Not ideal for:** Large enterprises requiring advanced sales features like territory management or lead scoring
- **Typical team size:** 10-50 employees with moderate customer interaction volumes
- **Time to value:** 1-2 weeks for basic setup, ongoing customization as needs evolve
- **Budget reality:** Affordable entry point with free trial, scales with team growth

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- **Pricing model:** Freemium with multiple paid tiers
- **Standout capability:** Proprietary relational database allowing complete data structure customization

## What the Tool Does

Attio fundamentally changes how teams think about CRM systems. Instead of adapting your business processes to fit a rigid CRM structure, Attio adapts to your business. The platform functions like sophisticated building blocks that can be arranged and rearranged to mirror your company's specific workflows, relationships, and data requirements.

Day-to-day, teams use Attio to manage contacts and deals through an interface that feels familiar to users of tools like Notion or Airtable, but with the power and depth of enterprise CRM functionality. The system automatically logs emails, tracks deal progression through customizable stages, and provides one-click data enrichment that pulls in unique business intelligence like funding rounds and annual recurring revenue data.

The platform's AI-native foundation means it anticipates user needs and automates routine tasks without requiring complex setup. Teams can create custom fields, define unique relationship types, and build workflows that reflect their actual business processes rather than generic sales funnels.

## Key Capabilities

### Modular Data Architecture
Attio's proprietary relational database model allows users to define custom data structures and relationships between contacts, companies, deals, and any other business entities. This means sales teams can track complex B2B relationships, partnerships, and multi-stakeholder deals in ways that traditional CRMs simply cannot accommodate. The system grows and evolves with your business needs rather than constraining them.

### Intelligent Contact Management
The platform excels at contact and company management with built-in email logging and automated data enrichment. One-click enrichment pulls in valuable business intelligence including company funding status, revenue estimates, and key personnel changes. This eliminates manual research and ensures your team always has current, actionable information about prospects and customers.

### Flexible Pipeline Management
Deal tracking in Attio goes beyond basic stage management. Teams can create custom sales stages, define unique metrics for different deal types, and build reporting dashboards that reflect their actual sales process. The visual interface makes it easy to spot bottlenecks, track conversion rates, and forecast revenue with accuracy.

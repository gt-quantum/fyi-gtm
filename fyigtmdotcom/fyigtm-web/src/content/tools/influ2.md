## What is Influ2?

Influ2 is the first person-based marketing platform designed specifically for B2B account-based marketing (ABM), enabling marketers to target individual decision-makers by name rather than broad audience segments. The platform solves the fundamental challenge of reaching specific high-value prospects across multiple advertising channels while providing detailed engagement tracking at the individual contact level. Its primary value proposition centers on bridging the gap between marketing activities and sales outcomes by delivering named prospect data that sales teams can act on immediately.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Enterprise B2B companies with deal sizes of $50k+ and established ABM strategies
- **Not ideal for:** Startups, SMBs with limited ad budgets, or companies with shorter sales cycles
- **Typical team size:** 50+ employees with dedicated marketing and sales teams
- **Time to value:** 3-6 months for campaign optimization, 7 months average ROI realization
- **Budget reality:** High-tier investment starting around $5 per engaged contact, with bundled media and software costs

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** ABM & Demand Generation
- **Pricing model:** Enterprise custom pricing
- **Standout capability:** Contact-level targeting and engagement tracking across multiple ad platforms

## What the Tool Does

Influ2 transforms traditional account-based marketing by allowing teams to upload lists of specific individuals they want to reach and then serve targeted advertisements to those exact people across LinkedIn, Facebook, Google, Amazon, and Instagram. Instead of hoping the right person sees your ad within a target company, you can ensure that the VP of Sales at your dream account receives your message directly.

The platform's workflow starts with importing your target contact lists, typically containing named decision-makers from your ideal customer profile accounts. Marketing teams then create campaigns targeting these specific individuals across multiple channels simultaneously. As these prospects interact with ads, Influ2's person-tracker captures detailed engagement data, including which specific people viewed, clicked, or engaged with content, even if they don't fill out forms.

This engagement intelligence flows directly to sales teams, providing them with warm conversation starters and behavioral insights about their prospects' interests. Sales reps can see exactly which ads their targets engaged with, when they were most active, and what content resonated, enabling more informed and timely outreach. The platform also provides account-level scoring and intent signals, helping prioritize follow-up activities based on engagement intensity.

## Key Capabilities

### Person-Level Targeting Across Channels
Influ2 enables marketers to target specific named individuals rather than demographic or firmographic segments across major advertising platforms. This precision targeting eliminates wasted ad spend on irrelevant audiences and ensures your message reaches the exact decision-makers you want to influence. The capability matters because it transforms advertising from a spray-and-pray approach into a surgical precision tool for ABM campaigns.

### Contact-Level Engagement Tracking
The platform's person-tracker identifies and tracks individual prospect interactions with marketing campaigns, providing names, companies, and behavioral scores for each engagement. This visibility into who specifically is engaging with your content, rather than anonymous visitor data, enables sales teams to prioritize outreach and personalize conversations. The tracking works even when prospects don't complete forms, capturing intent signals that would otherwise be lost.

### Sales-Marketing Alignment Intelligence
Influ2 bridges the traditional gap between marketing activities and sales outcomes by providing detailed interaction data directly to sales representatives. Sales teams receive notifications when their target accounts engage with marketing content, complete with context about which materials resonated and engagement intensity. This real-time intelligence enables timely, relevant outreach that leverages marketing touchpoints to advance sales conversations.

## Pricing Reality

Influ2 operates on a custom pricing model that bundles software platform access with media spend into a single flat fee, designed to simplify budgeting for enterprise marketing teams. The pricing structure is based primarily on the number of contacts you want to target rather than traditional cost-per-click or impression models.

- **Entry cost:** Significant enterprise-level investment, approximately $5 per contact that engages with campaigns
- **Scaling cost:** Pricing increases with the size of your target contact database and campaign scope
- **Contract terms:** Typically annual contracts with bundled software and media spend commitments

The platform positions itself as a premium solution, with pricing reflecting its enterprise focus. Teams should budget for substantial monthly investments, as the model charges for contact engagement rather than just platform access. While specific pricing isn't publicly disclosed, user reports suggest significant budget requirements that make it suitable primarily for companies with established ABM programs and substantial marketing investments.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros
- **Precision targeting:** Ability to reach specific named decision-makers rather than broad audience segments dramatically improves campaign efficiency and reduces wasted spend on irrelevant audiences
- **Actionable sales intelligence:** Sales teams receive detailed, named engagement data that enables timely, personalized outreach with clear conversation starters and context about prospect interests
- **Multi-channel coordination:** Orchestrating consistent messaging across LinkedIn, Facebook, Google, Amazon, and Instagram from a single platform ensures comprehensive coverage of target prospects

### Cons
- **High cost barrier:** Premium pricing model makes it inaccessible for smaller companies or those with limited marketing budgets, potentially pricing out mid-market organizations
- **Platform integration challenges:** Users report difficulties with CRM integrations and data manipulation capabilities that can create workflow friction and require additional manual processes
- **Ad blocking limitations:** Teams still pay for engagement attempts even when targets use strict ad filters, leading to budget inefficiencies when reaching privacy-conscious executives

## Verdict

**Bottom line:** Influ2 delivers unmatched precision in B2B targeting for enterprises willing to invest significantly in reaching specific decision-makers, but the premium pricing limits its accessibility to well-funded ABM programs.

The platform excels at solving the fundamental challenge of person-based marketing in B2B environments, providing capabilities that simply aren't available through traditional advertising platforms. For enterprise organizations with substantial deal sizes and established ABM strategies, the ability to target named individuals and track engagement at the contact level can justify the significant investment through improved conversion rates and shortened sales cycles. However, the high cost barrier and integration challenges mean smaller organizations or those new to ABM should consider building foundational capabilities with more accessible tools before graduating to Influ2's enterprise-focused approach.

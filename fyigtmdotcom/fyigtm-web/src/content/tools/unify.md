## What is Unify?

Unify is a comprehensive go-to-market platform that automates the entire outbound sales process by combining intent data monitoring, AI-powered research, and personalized engagement sequences. The platform solves the challenge of scaling warm outbound by identifying when prospects show buying intent and automatically executing targeted outreach campaigns that feel personal rather than mass-produced.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market to enterprise sales teams executing high-volume outbound campaigns
- **Not ideal for:** Small businesses or startups with limited budgets (under $700/month)
- **Typical team size:** 10+ person sales and marketing teams
- **Time to value:** 2-4 weeks for initial setup and workflow optimization
- **Budget reality:** Minimum $700/month commitment, realistically $1,000+ with additional users

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Sales Engagement Platform
- **Pricing model:** Paid only, no free tier
- **Standout capability:** Automated intent-driven prospecting workflows ("Plays")

## What the Tool Does

Unify operates as a centralized command center for outbound sales operations. The platform continuously monitors over 10 intent data sources to identify when target accounts show buying signals, then automatically triggers customized workflows called "Plays" that handle everything from prospect research to personalized email sequences.

In practice, teams set up Plays that define their ideal customer criteria and engagement strategies. When Unify detects intent signals matching these parameters, it automatically enriches prospect data using AI research, crafts personalized outreach messages based on company-specific insights, and launches multi-step email sequences. The system handles the heavy lifting of prospecting while maintaining the personal touch that makes warm outbound effective.

Sales reps receive qualified prospects with detailed research briefs and suggested talking points, allowing them to focus on high-value conversations rather than manual prospecting tasks. The platform integrates with existing CRM systems to ensure seamless workflow integration and accurate tracking of engagement outcomes.

## Key Capabilities

### Intent Signal Aggregation
- Monitors over 10 intent data sources to identify buying signals across target accounts
- Provides real-time alerts when prospects enter active buying cycles
- Enables teams to reach prospects at the optimal moment when they're researching solutions

### Automated Workflow Engine (Plays)
- Creates custom automation sequences that handle prospecting, enrichment, research, and outreach
- Combines multiple sales development tasks into single, repeatable workflows
- Allows teams to scale personalized outbound without proportional increases in manual effort

### AI-Powered Prospect Research
- Automatically researches prospects and companies to generate personalized insights
- Creates customized talking points and email content based on company-specific information
- Reduces the time reps spend on pre-call research while improving message relevance

## Pricing Reality

Unify follows a credit-based pricing model with significant upfront investment requirements. The entry-level Growth plan costs $700 per month when billed annually and includes 30,000 credits, three platform users, and one email-sending user.

- **Entry cost:** $700/month (annual commitment required)
- **Scaling cost:** Additional platform users cost $25/month, email-sending users cost $100/month, extra mailboxes cost $20/month
- **Contract terms:** Annual billing required, custom pricing for Pro tier

The credit system covers various platform activities, but the Growth plan only includes two Plays, which can be limiting for teams wanting to test multiple campaign strategies. Most growing teams will likely need the Pro tier for expanded functionality, though specific pricing isn't publicly available.

No free trial is mentioned, making it difficult for teams to evaluate fit before committing to the substantial monthly investment.

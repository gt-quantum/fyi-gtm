## What is Xactly?

Xactly is a comprehensive sales performance management (SPM) platform that helps companies design, automate, and optimize their incentive compensation programs. Founded in 2005, the platform uses AI-powered insights and 20+ years of industry benchmarking data to help revenue operations teams plan commission structures, motivate sales teams, and predict revenue outcomes with greater accuracy.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market to enterprise companies with complex commission structures and multiple sales teams
- **Not ideal for:** Small businesses with simple commission plans or startups with limited revenue operations needs
- **Typical team size:** 50+ employees with dedicated RevOps or sales operations teams
- **Time to value:** 3-6 months for full implementation and optimization
- **Budget reality:** Enterprise-level investment requiring custom pricing discussions

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Revenue Analytics & Attribution
- **Pricing model:** Enterprise with custom pricing
- **Standout capability:** AI-driven commission plan simulation and benchmarking against industry data

## What the Tool Does

Xactly transforms how companies manage sales compensation by automating the entire incentive lifecycle. The platform connects directly to your CRM, ERP, and other business systems to pull in sales data, automatically calculate commissions based on predefined rules, and provide real-time visibility into earnings and performance metrics.

On a day-to-day basis, sales reps log into personalized dashboards to track their commission progress, view detailed earning statements, and understand exactly how their activities translate to compensation. Meanwhile, sales managers and RevOps teams use the platform's analytics capabilities to monitor plan performance, identify optimization opportunities, and make data-driven adjustments to incentive structures.

The platform's AI engine continuously analyzes compensation data across thousands of companies, providing benchmarking insights that help organizations design more effective incentive plans. This means teams can simulate the impact of plan changes before implementation, reducing the risk of unintended consequences on sales behavior.

## Key Capabilities

### Flexible Compensation Plan Design
Xactly's plan builder allows revenue teams to create and manage any type of commission structure, from simple percentage-based plans to complex multi-tiered arrangements with accelerators, caps, and team-based components. The platform uses reusable elements like rules, quotas, and rate tables, making it easier to maintain consistency across different sales roles while accommodating unique requirements for specific teams or territories.

### Automated Commission Processing
The platform eliminates manual commission calculations by automatically processing payouts based on real-time sales data. This automation reduces processing time dramatically, with some users reporting reductions from three weeks of manual work to just one day of automated processing. The system handles complex scenarios like split commissions, deal modifications, and retroactive adjustments without manual intervention.

### Real-Time Performance Analytics
Xactly provides over 100 out-of-the-box reports plus customizable dashboards that give both individual contributors and managers visibility into performance metrics. Sales reps can see exactly where they stand against quota, track commission earnings in real-time, and understand how different deal scenarios will impact their compensation. Managers get aggregate views of team performance, plan effectiveness, and potential payout projections.

## Pricing Reality

Xactly operates on a custom enterprise pricing model, which means costs vary significantly based on company size, complexity of compensation plans, and required features. The platform doesn't publish standard pricing, instead offering personalized quotes after consultation calls.

- **Entry cost:** Likely five-figure annual commitments for mid-market implementations
- **Scaling cost:** Pricing typically increases with user count and plan complexity
- **Contract terms:** Annual contracts are standard, with multi-year agreements common for enterprise deals

The platform offers demos and proof-of-concept implementations to help prospects evaluate fit before committing. Organizations should budget for implementation services and training, as the platform requires significant configuration to match existing compensation structures.

## Pros & Cons

### Pros
- **Dramatic time savings:** Users consistently report massive reductions in commission processing time, with some cutting three-week manual processes down to single days
- **Handles complex scenarios:** The platform excels at managing intricate commission structures that would be nearly impossible to handle accurately in spreadsheets
- **Real-time visibility:** Sales teams appreciate having instant access to commission data and performance tracking, eliminating the quarterly "commission mystery"

### Cons
- **Complex initial setup:** Multiple users report challenging implementation periods, with rule configuration and system setup requiring significant technical expertise
- **Reporting limitations:** While the platform offers many reports, users note that customizing analytics and dashboards can be difficult and time-consuming
- **Occasional performance issues:** Some users experience data update lags and slower system performance, particularly during high-volume processing periods

## What is Drift by Salesloft?

Drift is a conversational marketing platform designed for B2B companies that transforms website visitors into qualified pipeline through AI-powered conversations. The platform combines live chat, intelligent chatbots, and personalized engagement tools to help sales and marketing teams capture leads, book meetings, and accelerate revenue in real time. Following Salesloft's acquisition of Drift in February 2024, the platform now integrates deeply with sales engagement workflows to create a unified buyer journey from first touch to closed deal.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Not ideal for:** Small businesses or teams with limited budgets under $30K annually
- **Typical team size:** 50+ employees with dedicated sales and marketing functions
- **Time to value:** 30-60 days for full implementation and optimization
- **Budget reality:** Expect $2,500-$10,000+ monthly investment for meaningful functionality

- **Category:** Conversational Marketing & Lead Management

## What the Tool Does

Drift operates as your website's intelligent sales assistant, engaging visitors the moment they arrive and guiding them through personalized conversations. The platform uses reverse IP lookup to identify company visitors, then deploys targeted messaging based on firmographic data and behavioral signals. When a high-intent prospect engages, Drift's AI qualifies them through preset questions, captures essential information, and either routes them to the appropriate sales rep or books a meeting directly in their calendar.

The system monitors visitor behavior across your site, triggering conversations at optimal moments when prospects show buying intent. For enterprise accounts, the platform can surface account-level insights that help sales teams personalize their approach before the first human interaction occurs. This creates a seamless handoff from marketing to sales that preserves context and accelerates deal progression.

## Key Capabilities

### AI-Powered Conversation Engine
The platform's chatbots use GPT-powered AI to engage in natural conversations with website visitors, providing accurate answers to complex questions and guiding prospects through qualification flows. The system comes with 60+ conversation topics ready to deploy on day one, covering common B2B scenarios like product demos, pricing discussions, and technical questions. Companies report that these AI conversations feel authentic enough that prospects often don't realize they're chatting with a bot initially.

### Smart Lead Qualification and Routing
Drift automatically qualifies inbound leads by asking strategic questions about company size, budget, timeline, and specific pain points. The platform then routes qualified prospects to the right sales rep based on account ownership, territory rules, or team availability. High-intent visitors can book meetings instantly through calendar integrations, while lower-priority leads flow into nurture campaigns. This intelligent routing ensures hot prospects connect with sales reps within minutes rather than days.

### Account-Based Engagement
The platform excels at identifying and engaging target accounts through firmographic data and behavioral tracking. Sales teams receive real-time notifications when key accounts visit the website, including details about which pages they viewed and how long they engaged. This intelligence allows reps to craft personalized outreach that references the prospect's specific interests and timing, significantly improving connection rates and meeting acceptance.

## Pricing Reality

Drift operates on an enterprise pricing model that reflects its position as a premium conversational marketing platform. The entry point is steep compared to basic chat tools, but the pricing aligns with the platform's sophisticated capabilities and B2B focus.

- **Entry cost:** $2,500/month for Premium plan (billed annually)
- **Scaling cost:** Advanced and Enterprise plans require custom pricing based on traffic volume and feature requirements
- **Contract terms:** Annual commitments required; month-to-month options not available

The Premium plan includes custom chatbots, live chat, conversational landing pages, and basic routing capabilities. Higher tiers add advanced features like A/B testing, sophisticated routing logic, and enhanced AI capabilities. Most mid-market companies should budget $3,000-$6,000 monthly, while enterprise implementations often exceed $10,000 monthly when factoring in additional Salesloft integrations.

## What is Enterprise Revenue Orchestration?

Clari's Enterprise Revenue Orchestration is an AI-powered platform that centralizes revenue operations by connecting CRM data, email communications, calendar activities, and sales calls into a unified dashboard. The platform uses machine learning to analyze deal patterns and provide predictive insights, helping revenue teams spot risks early and take proactive action to prevent revenue leakage.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market to enterprise B2B companies with complex sales cycles and multiple revenue streams
- **Not ideal for:** Small teams under 50 people or organizations with simple, transactional sales processes
- **Typical team size:** 100+ employees with dedicated revenue operations functions
- **Time to value:** 3-6 months for full implementation and meaningful insights
- **Budget reality:** Significant enterprise investment, likely $50,000+ annually for most implementations

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Pipeline & Forecasting
- **Pricing model:** Custom enterprise pricing
- **Standout capability:** AI-powered predictive intelligence that analyzes millions of deal patterns

## What the Tool Does

Clari functions as a "single pane of glass" for revenue leaders who need to understand their entire sales pipeline without jumping between multiple systems. The platform automatically ingests data from your existing tech stack, then applies AI analysis to surface insights that would be impossible to detect manually. Revenue leaders can see real-time pipeline health, get accurate forecasts, and receive specific recommendations about which deals need attention and why.

The system works by continuously monitoring deal progression signals across all connected systems. When a deal shows warning signs like decreased email engagement, missed meetings, or stalled progression, Clari flags it for immediate attention. The platform also identifies positive signals that indicate deals are likely to accelerate or close ahead of schedule, helping teams allocate resources more effectively.

## Key Capabilities

### Predictive Intelligence
Clari's AI analyzes patterns across millions of deals to forecast outcomes with greater accuracy than traditional methods. The system learns from historical data to identify early warning signs of deal risk and opportunity acceleration. Revenue leaders receive specific predictions about which deals will likely close, which are at risk, and what actions can improve outcomes. This capability transforms reactive pipeline management into proactive revenue orchestration.

### Revenue Cadences
The platform establishes structured governance processes that ensure consistent execution across revenue teams. Revenue Cadences guide teams through systematic review processes, ensuring critical activities happen on schedule and nothing falls through the cracks. This feature brings discipline to revenue operations by creating repeatable workflows that scale across large organizations. Teams get clear guidance on where to focus attention and how to execute effectively.

### Smart Automation Features
Advanced AI capabilities including Ask Clari (natural language querying), Smart Chapters (automated call summaries), and Smart Follow Ups reduce manual work while improving accuracy. These features eliminate tedious administrative tasks that typically consume revenue team bandwidth. The automation learns from user behavior and deal patterns to provide increasingly relevant recommendations over time.

## Pricing Reality

Clari uses completely custom enterprise pricing with no publicly available rate cards. Costs vary significantly based on organization size, operational complexity, user count, and specific module selection.

- **Entry cost:** Likely $50,000+ annually for mid-market implementations
- **Scaling cost:** Pricing increases with user count and additional modules (Forecasting, Execution, Platform bundle)
- **Contract terms:** Annual commitments standard, with multi-year agreements common for enterprise deals

The platform offers demos and proof-of-concept engagements before full implementation. Organizations should budget for integration costs and change management beyond the software licensing fees.

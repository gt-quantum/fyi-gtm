Descript is a revolutionary video and podcast editing platform that lets you edit media by editing text transcripts.

## What is Descript?

Descript transforms video and audio editing by providing automatic transcription and allowing you to edit your media simply by editing the transcript. Cut a word from the text, and it's cut from the video.

## Key Features

- Text-based video editing
- Automatic transcription
- Overdub voice cloning
- Screen recording
- Multi-track editing
- Filler word removal

## Pricing

Descript offers a free tier with basic features. Creator plans start at $15/month with more transcription hours and features.

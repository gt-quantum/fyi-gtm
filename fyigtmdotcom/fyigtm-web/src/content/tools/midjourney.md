Midjourney is an AI image generation tool that creates stunning artwork from text descriptions. It's known for its distinctive artistic style and high-quality outputs.

## What is Midjourney?

Midjourney is a generative AI program that creates images from natural language descriptions called prompts. It's operated through a Discord bot and has become one of the most popular AI art generators available.

## Key Features

- High-quality artistic image generation
- Multiple style variations per prompt
- Upscaling and variation options
- Community gallery for inspiration
- Regular model updates with improved quality
- Pan and zoom features for image expansion

## Pricing

Midjourney offers subscription plans starting at $10/month for the Basic plan, with higher tiers offering more GPU time and features.

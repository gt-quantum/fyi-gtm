## What is Dreamdata?

Dreamdata is a B2B attribution and activation platform that solves one of marketing's most persistent challenges: understanding which touchpoints actually drive revenue. The platform creates comprehensive customer journey maps by tracking every buyer interaction across channels, then uses AI to identify intent signals and optimize marketing spend based on what actually converts prospects into customers.

## Decision Snapshot

- **Best for:** Mid-market to enterprise B2B companies with complex, multi-touch sales cycles
- **Not ideal for:** Small businesses with simple funnels or companies primarily focused on B2C marketing  
- **Typical team size:** Marketing teams of 5+ at companies with $10M+ annual revenue
- **Time to value:** 2-4 weeks for initial insights, 2-3 months for full optimization
- **Budget reality:** Minimum $750/month for meaningful features, often $2,000+ for enterprise needs

## Quick Facts

- **Category:** Revenue Analytics & Attribution
- **Pricing model:** Freemium with enterprise tiers
- **Standout capability:** Complete B2B customer journey mapping with account-level attribution

## What the Tool Does

Dreamdata works by collecting touchpoint data from across your marketing and sales stack, then organizing it into account-level journeys that show how prospects actually move through your funnel. Instead of relying on last-touch attribution that credits only the final interaction, or first-touch models that ignore nurturing efforts, Dreamdata maps the entire customer journey from anonymous visitor to closed deal.

The platform automatically resolves anonymous website visitors to their companies using IP tracking, then connects their behavior to your CRM data. When someone from that account later converts, Dreamdata can trace back through every blog post they read, every ad they clicked, and every email they opened to build a complete picture of what influenced the sale.

Marketing teams use these insights daily to make budget allocation decisions, identify which content actually drives pipeline, and build audiences for retargeting campaigns. The platform also sends real-time alerts when high-value accounts show buying intent, allowing sales and marketing to coordinate immediate outreach.

## Key Capabilities

### Complete Journey Mapping
Dreamdata tracks every touchpoint from first anonymous visit to closed deal, organizing individual interactions into account-level journeys. This matters because B2B purchases involve multiple stakeholders who research across different channels over months. Instead of seeing disconnected data points, marketers get a coherent story of how accounts actually convert, enabling them to replicate successful patterns and eliminate wasteful spending.

### AI-Powered Intent Signals  
The platform uses artificial intelligence to analyze visitor behavior patterns and identify when accounts are showing purchase intent. It automatically triggers notifications when key accounts visit your pricing page multiple times, download competitive content, or exhibit other high-intent behaviors. This allows marketing and sales teams to strike while prospects are actively evaluating solutions, dramatically improving conversion rates.

### Precise Audience Building and Activation
Marketers can build highly specific audiences using unlimited filtering options, then automatically sync them to major advertising platforms like Google Ads, Facebook, and LinkedIn. For example, you might create an audience of accounts that visited your enterprise features page but haven't requested a demo, then serve them targeted ads highlighting customer success stories. This precision targeting helps maximize ad spend efficiency and improve campaign performance.

## Pricing Reality

Dreamdata operates on a freemium model with significant limitations on the free tier. The free plan includes basic account-level tracking and revenue analytics but lacks the advanced features most B2B companies need.

- **Entry cost:** $750/month for the Activation Starter plan with expanded integrations and data volume
- **Scaling cost:** Advanced Attribution tier requires enterprise pricing (typically $2,000+ monthly)
- **Contract terms:** Annual commitments common, with setup fees for complex integrations

The free trial provides enough functionality to test basic attribution, but serious evaluation requires the paid tier. Many users report the learning curve means you need several months to see full value, making the annual commitment more palatable despite the high entry cost.

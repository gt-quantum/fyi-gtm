Notion AI brings artificial intelligence directly into your Notion workspace, helping you write, brainstorm, and organize more effectively.

## What is Notion AI?

Notion AI is an integrated AI assistant within the Notion platform. It can help draft content, summarize notes, generate action items, translate text, and enhance your writing without leaving your workspace.

## Key Features

- In-document AI writing assistance
- Content summarization
- Action item extraction
- Translation capabilities
- Brainstorming and ideation
- Writing improvement suggestions

## Pricing

Notion AI is available as an add-on at $10 per member per month on top of your Notion subscription.
